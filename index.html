Welcome!

Welcome to my webpage! My name is Owen Bench, and I'm currently a student at Davis Technical College in Utah, as well as a high school student at Northern Utah Academy for Math, Enginnering & Science (NUAMES). This page was made to hold my e-portfolio and as a project for a Davis Tech course. I will regularly update this page both as my portfolio expands and as my course requires edits to the page. This webpage has two main purposes which you know already: To house my portfolio and get comissions from. Commissions for what you might ask? Here's what I do comissions for:

Writings
Depending on the content, I can either help with the writing process or write content for you. I will not take commissions for writing papers or essays for schooling, I will only give pointers and advice for these types of writing.
Programming
I will write programs or build webpages for you. The cost of a commission will depend on the length and complexity of the program. If you want a simple Auto Hotkey program to be made, say to automatically write files into an Excel sheet, that would be the cheaper end. On the higher end would be things like small games or large webpages.
Covers
I currently play drums as a hobby, and will do commissions for covers of songs. Plain and simple. Cost will again depend on length and complexity and I will negotiate what I feel would be a fair price for the cover.
If you're interested in doing a commission, go to the commissions page for further information. Taking any content that was commissioned and claiming it as your own is plagiarism, so please either ask permission before posting commissions or give credit in the posts.
