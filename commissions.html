Writings

Writing can be anything from spell/grammar checking, tutoring, and writing of anything you might want. This does not include academic papers or anything intended to be used and passed off as your own work. Any writing commission sent is made with that in mind, and that reuse of my work unless given word will be considered plagiarism.

Programming

Programming can be any tutoring, aid, second opinion, or creation you might want. This does not include anything meant to pass of as one's own work, like academic or professional work. Give credit where it's due if you want to reuse it. Price will vary depending on program length and complexity.

Covers

Covers can be any piece of music that I can play drums to. Whether it be trying to faithfully recreate a part or creating parts for your song. Price will vary depending on song length and complexity, which I will negotiate.
